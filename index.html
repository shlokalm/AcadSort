About Us

Methodology
How We Actually Work

Classes are entirely online, with all the teachers having iPad setups. Classes are kept very interactive, freeing the student to ask any doubts. At the end of each lesson, a pdf of class notes is given to students along with some dedicated assignments after each class. Students are supposed to send us a pdf of their work before the next class, and all the HWs are checked thoroughly by teachers. We also conduct weekly tests to ensure that students are on a steep learning curve with us. Tests and assignments are ways to understand students' understanding; we study student progress rigorously through these and try to identify the gaps in the learning. Subsequent lessons are planned in accordance with that. Students are always encouraged to think rather than spoon feeding in the sessions; this ensures the long-term success of students.
